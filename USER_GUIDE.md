## ðŸ› ï¸ What Gas Genie Can Do (The 8 Tools)

Gas Genie is equipped with 8 specialized tools that connect directly to your database, Google Calendar, and Gmail. Here is a breakdown of what you can ask it to do, along with example prompts.

### 1. Log a New Job
Gas Genie can create a new job record in the database, automatically generating a job number (e.g., `JOB-001`). It captures customer details, addresses, and issue descriptions.
> **Try saying:** 
> - *"Hi Genie, please log a new job for Mrs. Smith at 42 Oak Road. She has a Vaillant ecoTEC with an F28 error code."*
> - *"I need to create a high-priority job for John Doe. His phone number is 07700 900123, and the boiler is leaking."*

### 2. Update Job Notes
You can add timestamped notes to an existing job while you are on site, keeping your admin up to date without typing.
> **Try saying:**
> - *"Update the notes for job number 012. Say: Arrived on site, found the diverter valve is passing, will need to order a new one."*
> - *"Add a note to JOB-005 that the customer wasn't home."*

### 3. Diagnose Boiler Error Codes
Gas Genie has a built-in database of 29 common error codes across 8 major manufacturers (Vaillant, Worcester Bosch, Baxi, Ideal, Potterton, Glow-worm, Viessmann, Ferroli). It tells you the likely cause severity, and fix steps.
> **Try saying:**
> - *"What does an F28 error mean on a Vaillant?"*
> - *"Look up error EA on a Worcester Bosch."*

### 4. Look Up Parts & Pricing
Instantly check prices, stock availability, and supplier info for common gas parts.
> **Try saying:**
> - *"How much is a diverter valve for an Ideal Logic?"*
> - *"Can you check the price and stock for an auto air vent?"*

### 5. Check Gas Regulations
Gas Genie has instant access to key UK gas safety regulations and Building Regs.
> **Try saying:**
> - *"What are the flue termination distance rules next to an opening window?"*
> - *"Remind me of the ventilation requirements for a 15 kilowatt open-flued boiler."*
> - *"What is the procedure for an Immediately Dangerous unsafe situation?"*

### 6. Check Your Calendar Availability
Gas Genie integrates with your Google Calendar to check your schedule for free slots, handling relative dates like "tomorrow" or "next Monday".
> **Try saying:**
> - *"When is my next free slot tomorrow?"*
> - *"Check my availability for next Wednesday."*

### 7. Book an Appointment
Gas Genie can create a new event directly in your Google Calendar with the customer's details and address.
> **Try saying:**
> - *"Book an appointment for Mrs. Smith tomorrow at 9 AM at 42 Oak Road."*
> - *"Schedule a service for John Doe on the 15th of March at 2 PM."*

### 8. Send Booking Confirmations
Gas Genie can send an automated email confirmation to the customer via your Gmail account.
> **Try saying:**
> - *"Can you send a booking confirmation email to test@example.com for Mrs. Smith's appointment tomorrow at 9 AM?"*
